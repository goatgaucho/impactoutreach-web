## The Client Situation

FNFFS (Coalition of First Nations for Finfish Stewardship) is running a 60-day campaign to push the federal government to reverse the 2029 ban on open-net-pen salmon farming in BC. Targets are BC Liberal MPs (including FOPO Chair Patrick Weiler) plus Liberal MPs in food-affordability ridings. Leverage window is the 2026 two-year licence review. Client wants to run it on New/Mode. Pitch stage, not yet signed.

## The Core Idea

New/Mode is a strong delivery platform. It handles the plumbing. But it's a general-purpose advocacy tool, not a purpose-built campaign accelerator for this fight.

Everything we're proposing takes what New/Mode already does well and multiplies its effectiveness for this campaign. Nothing we build replaces New/Mode. Everything we build makes it hit harder.

New/Mode gives you a petition form. We give FNFFS a campaign. New/Mode sends email to MPs. We make sure every email is the sharpest version of that message for that specific MP. New/Mode has an LTE tool. We turn it into a precision instrument that writes per-publication drafts and keeps them out of each other's way.

Required tier on the client's New/Mode subscription is Movement Builder+, **$709 CAD per month** month-to-month.

## MVP Scope (Included)

For each item, what New/Mode does natively, what we add, why it accelerates.

**1. FNFFS-branded campaign website.** New/Mode gives a hosted form with their template. We build a real branded site with narrative pages, ad-optimized landing pages, and owned SEO presence. Ads convert multiple times better on purpose-built pages than on platform forms.

**2. FNFFS Operations Console.** New/Mode has its own admin dashboard. We add the two pieces New/Mode cannot do for FNFFS: the voice approval workflow (Coalition reviews generated content before push) and the targeting intelligence view (prioritized MP list with persuadability scoring). Scoped narrow and additive, not duplicating New/Mode's native dashboard.

**3. Targeting intelligence layer.** New/Mode routes by postcode to whatever MP the supporter has. We provide a curated priority list of the MPs that actually matter: FOPO Liberals, the Minister, thin-margin BC Liberals, Atlantic fishing Liberals. Tiered and scored. Routes pressure to where pressure matters. Appendix A in the full doc.

**4. AI email writer tuned for FNFFS voice.** New/Mode's "AI message variation" rotates a single template in different voices. We add per-recipient, context-aware composition. Every email to Weiler cites FOPO. Every email to Hanley cites Yukon food costs. This is the single most important piece of intellectual value in the engagement.

**5. 30-day drip scheduler.** New/Mode doesn't do native multi-day drip cadence. We add sustained-pressure scheduling with 45-minute spacing, business-hours randomization, subject-line rotation. Keeps MP inboxes lit up for weeks, looks organic from every angle.

**6. LTE tool amplification.** New/Mode has a real LTE tool at Movement Builder+. We add the intellectual layer: pre-drafted, publication-tailored letters generated by GPT-4o, duplicate-subject protection, same voice approval workflow as email. LTEs that actually run in the Times Colonist are worth more than a hundred that get deleted.

**7. New/Mode V2 API integration.** The connective tissue. OAuth2, submission push, schema discovery, analytics polling. Not flashy but it makes everything else possible.

**8. Reply capture and campaign health analytics.** New/Mode shows sends and opens. We show whether MPs are actually responding and whether we're right about who's movable.

**9. Supporter voice approval workflow.** The control point that makes "AI-generated" and "authentic" compatible in the same campaign. Hard block in the system, not convention.

**10. Deployment, hosting, FNFFS staff training.** Table stakes.

**Timeline**: 6 to 8 weeks kickoff to first production send.

## Additional Scope Options (Phase 2, costs extra $$)

Modular. Each adds to what New/Mode natively provides.

- **Rapid-response trigger system.** Monitors DFO, FOPO, opposition activity, auto-drafts responses. *New/Mode has nothing in this category.*
- **Digital ad creative generator.** Riding-specific variants, Meta/Google Ads integration. *New/Mode has no ad tooling.*
- **"Which Nations Decide?" interactive map.** Reframes the "120 First Nations support the ban" counter-narrative as a jurisdictional question. *Single highest-leverage Phase 2 piece. Jessie, push this if the client has any appetite at all.*
- **Analytics and optimization dashboard.** Deeper than MVP, for clients actively optimizing mid-campaign.
- **Expanded LTE distribution.** Regional papers beyond New/Mode's core database.
- **Storytelling engine.** Supporter voice capture feeding ad and LTE creative.
- **Counter-narrative monitoring.** Opposition org scraping and alerts. Pairs with rapid-response.
